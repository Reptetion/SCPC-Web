Our Services

Our services provide expert bookkeeping tailored for businesses, charities, and condo boards!

Bookkeeping

We take care of your daily financial transactions, from reconciliations to expense categorization, so you can focus on growing your business. Your books stay current, accurate, and audit-ready every month.

CRA Compliance & Audit Support

With us, we ensure your records are organized, transparent, and fully aligned with CRA requirements. If you’re ever reviewed or audited, we’re here to provide documentation and support every step of the way.

Financial Reporting & Insights

Get clear, customized financial reports like income statements, balance sheets, and cash flow summaries. Delivered monthly or quarterly, our reports help you understand your numbers and make confident business decisions.

Payroll Services

We handle payroll from start to finish — processing pay runs, calculating deductions, and preparing year-end tax slips. Your team gets paid correctly and on time, with full CRA compliance guaranteed.

Restricted Fund & Grant Tracking

We help you track restricted donations, grants, and casino funds with precision and transparency. This service is especially important for charities and nonprofits managing multiple funding sources.

Year-End & T3010 Filing

Whether you're a charity, small business, or condo board, we prepare accurate year-end financials and ensure your records are properly closed. We also complete and file T3010 returns for registered charities.
